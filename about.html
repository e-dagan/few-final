About
Hello I'm Elena... I am an interdisciplinary maker living between NYC, San Francisco, and Shanghai. Following my curiosities in creative tech, photography, and product design, I find value working across multiple mediums and industries. I'm also excited to challenge myself with new projects that especially uplift voices in the Asian American and LGBTQ+ communities. Currently, I am photographing at various NYC film festivals and film sets including the New York Asian Film Festival and Asian American International Film Festival. This year, I will finish my degree at NYU Shanghai studying Interactive Media Arts.
